History

What have we been up to?

  • October 2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    This was one of our first meetings in Halls in First Year, we cooked Spanish tortilla and tapas and brought our community together, from then on its been NONSTOP!

  • October 2015

    A Society is Born

    We took part in the Freshers Fair trying to sign up as many members as possible Spanish, Greek, Italian, Chinese, Singaporian, fromm all over the world were people trying tortilla and playing catch the apple!

  • November 2015

    Bringing Societies in London TOGETHER

    This was the first event where we brought together spanish societies from different unis in London, KCL, LSE, UCL, ICL all danced through the night to spanish REGGAETON!

  • February 2016

    SANGRIA NIGHT

    A night where we all met together to enjoy Spanish Reggaeton and bond with the Spanish people at Imperial, plus there was Sangria - Spanish drink ALL NIGHT!
